Our History

Persatuan Penduduk Taman Bunga Negara was established to strengthen relationships among residents and promote a safe, caring, and active neighbourhood. Since its formation, the association has organised various social, welfare, and community-based activities.

Vision

To build a harmonious, united, and well-connected community that prioritises the well-being of all residents.

Mission

  • To encourage unity and cooperation among residents
  • To organise meaningful social and community activities
  • To improve communication between residents and committee members
  • To promote a safe and clean living environment
